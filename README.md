# Acadia AI

> Turn unstructured information into actionable deadlines, tasks, and priorities using AI.

Acadia AI is an AI-powered information intelligence platform that helps users never miss important deadlines, tasks, announcements, or action items.

It processes content from emails, WhatsApp messages, PDFs, screenshots, notices, and other documents, automatically extracting important information and organizing it into a unified dashboard.

---

## Problem

Important information is scattered across multiple platforms:

- WhatsApp groups
- Emails
- PDFs
- Screenshots
- Circulars
- Internal portals
- Notices

People often miss:

- Deadlines
- Registration dates
- Assignment submissions
- Exam schedules
- Fee payments
- Events
- Follow-up actions

Keeping track of everything manually is inefficient and error-prone.

---

## Solution

Acadia AI uses AI-powered information extraction to identify and organize important information from unstructured content.

Users can:

- Paste text directly from messages or emails
- Upload PDFs
- Upload screenshots and images
- Extract deadlines automatically
- Categorize notices intelligently
- Prioritize important actions
- Query their data using an AI assistant

---

## Features

### Paste-to-Extract

Paste any message, email, notice, or announcement.

Example:

```text
Placement registration for XYZ Technologies closes on June 20.
Eligible branches: CSE, AIML.
Assessment begins June 25.
```

Acadia AI extracts:

- Title
- Category
- Deadline
- Priority
- Description

---

### AI Notice Dashboard

Automatically organizes extracted information into:

- Upcoming deadlines
- High-priority notices
- Events
- Exams
- Placements
- Assignments

---

### AI Assistant

Ask questions like:

- What deadlines do I have this week?
- What should I prioritize today?
- Summarize my notices.
- Did I miss anything important?
